What is Digital Legacy Planning?

Your digital life is more than just photos — it's financial assets, email accounts, smart home security, and personal memories. If you were unable to access your accounts tomorrow, would your family know how to?

I help you build a system to secure your digital footprint so your family inherits a clear roadmap when you're gone.
