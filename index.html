Frequently Asked Questions

Do you only help seniors?

I specialize in helping seniors, but I welcome anyone who wants patient, jargon-free tech support. My approach works especially well for people who feel intimidated by technology or have been frustrated by impatient help in the past.

Do I need to bring my device to you?

No — I come to you! I provide in-home service throughout North York and Willowdale. We can work in your home where you're comfortable, with your own Wi-Fi and setup.

What if you can't fix my problem?

You don't pay if you're not satisfied. That's my guarantee. If I can't help you, there's no charge for my time.

How long does a typical visit take?

Most issues are resolved in 1-2 hours. Simple problems like email setup might take 45 minutes, while more complex tasks like organizing years of photos could take 2-3 hours. I'll give you an honest estimate upfront.

Do you help with Apple products?

Yes! I'm an Apple specialist and help many seniors with iPhone, iPad, and Mac setup and troubleshooting. I can also help with Windows computers, Android phones, printers, and Wi-Fi networks.

Can you help protect me from scams?

Absolutely. Scam protection is one of my most requested services. I can teach you how to recognize scams, set up security on your devices, and provide ongoing monitoring through my Monthly Tech Care plan.
